Interview Rounds

  • Aptitude & Logical

    The first test! It checks your basic math, logic, and English skills.

  • Core IT Subjects

    Your college basics! Start by studying Databases, Operating Systems, and OOPs.

  • Coding Round

    Write actual code to solve problems. Practice basic logic and everyday coding challenges.

  • HR Interview

    A friendly chat! They just want to check your speaking skills and see if you are a good team player.

What Companies Want

DSA Mastery
Solve coding problems every day. Be confident writing code on your own.
Real Projects
Build 2 or 3 real apps that actually work. Avoid just copying old tutorial videos.
Clean Resume
Keep your resume to only 1 page. Make it very simple, neat, and honest.
Communication
Speak clearly! Always explain what you are thinking while you write your code.
Eager to Learn
Always be ready to learn! Show them you are excited to learn new things fast.
Dev Basics
Know the basics like Git, GitHub, simple APIs, and basic command line typing.

Modern AI Toolkit Essential

  • Smart Prompts

    Give clear commands. Tell the AI exactly what you need it to do.

  • Fixing Errors

    When your code breaks, paste the error to the AI and ask, "Why did this happen?"

  • Learning Fast

    Are you stuck? Ask the AI to teach you hard topics like you are 10 years old.

  • Practice Interviews

    Tell the AI to act like your boss or interviewer and ask you questions.

Golden Rule: Use AI like a smart teacher to learn fast. Do not just blindly copy and paste code.
